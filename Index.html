Faq-Questions

What species of hybrid cocoa do you sell?

We supply the TC Hybrid Series (TC1–TC8) developed by the Cocoa Research Institute of Nigeria (CRIN). These hybrids are known for:
* High yield: 1.5–2.0 tons/ha
* Early bearing potential
* Resistance to Phytophthora pod rot (black pod disease) and mirid (Sahlbergella singularis) attack
* High butter-fat content: at least 55%
* Superior cocoa flavour and excellent chocolate quality
* Excellent physical bean quality: >1.0 g in line with world market standards

When do the hybrid cocoa seedlings start fruiting?

Our TC hybrid seedlings begin fruiting within 2–3 years, provided that all Good Agricultural Practices (GAP) are properly followed.
You can learn more about GAP on our social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, and X/Twitter).

What is your farm and office location?

We are located in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria.

How much is transportation?

Transportation costs are handled by the customer. You may:
* Bring your own vehicle, or
* Charter a vehicle to our location for pickup, or
* Request that we link you with trusted drivers we have used in the past, who deliver to various locations across Nigeria.
Note: Negotiation of transport fees is done directly between the customer and the driver

What is your mode of payment?

Payment is usually made on the pickup day, immediately after loading your seedlings at our farm/pick-up location. However:
* You can pay before pickup if you want to secure your order.
* If payment has not been made before the agreed pickup date, the seedlings remain open for sale to other buyers.
* We honour and keep produce only for customers who have made a down payment or full early payment.
Most customers prefer to pay on the day of pickup, right after loading.
